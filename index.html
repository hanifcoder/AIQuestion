However, human intelligence has not yet achieved AGI, Artificial General Intelligence e.g. Do anything a human can do. Data is the backbone of AI. Data is unique to our professional and personal life. Apps keep updating due to changes in data. Acquiring data means collecting, labeling, and feeding it into AI. However, Data is messy. For example, if we are not careful in handling data it is like Garbage in, garbage out. For example, there are two main data problems: Incorrect Data and Missing Values. Also, there are multiple types of data including structured and unstructured data. The key point to keep in mind is that data is transforming our lives. Therefore, we need to be intentional and well-prepared to use it effectively.
